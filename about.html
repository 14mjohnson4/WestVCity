Why Placemaking?

The Inspiration Behind our Work!

For the Love of Cities...

"The mutual love affair between people and their place is one of the most powerful influences in our lives, yet rarely thought of in terms of a relationship. As cities begin thinking of themselves as engaged in a relationship with their citizens, and citizens begin to consider their emotional connections with their places, we open up new possibilities in community, social and economic development by including the most powerful of motivators—the human heart—in our toolkit of city-making."

Improving Communities!

It doesn't cost a fortune to improve our community, town, or city. As we are creative, we can come up with meaningful art and other designs that impact people and enhance their relationship with the city in which they live.

Adding some magic!

Every human has a deep longing for home and a sense of place. But home is not where many people think it is, because home is a feeling­­ – not a location. It is vital to help ordinary citizens add magic and soul to the public spaces in their neighborhood or town center. Nothing gives greater joy than working with communities to breathe new life into dead public spaces and dead shopping streets.
